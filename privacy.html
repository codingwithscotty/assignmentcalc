Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 17, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website and the associated mobile application, Assignment Grade Calculator ("the App").

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We respect your privacy. The App and the website:

  • Do not collect, store, or share any personal information.
  • Do not require user registration or login.
  • Do not access any sensitive device permissions or personal data.
  • Do not track your location or behavior.

2. Local Data Storage

All data entered into the App (such as grades or assignments) is stored locally on your device. This means:

  • Your data is not sent to any server.
  • We do not have access to any of the information you input.
  • If you uninstall the App or clear your browser data, your locally stored data may be permanently deleted.

3. Third-Party Services

The App and website do not use third-party services (e.g., analytics tools, advertising networks, or external databases) that collect or process personal information.

4. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any data, the App is safe for use by individuals of all ages, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

5. Security

While we do not store any user data, we take reasonable steps to ensure the App and website are secure and free from vulnerabilities. However, we recommend users keep their devices updated and secure.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. As we do not store user contact information, we will not notify users individually.
